About Me

Dedicated Public Servant

Temjenmenba Pongen, born in 1974, is a dedicated public servant representing the Jangpetkong constituency in Nagaland. With a Master's degree in Good Governance from Global Open University, Dimapur, he has been instrumental in driving educational and infrastructural development in the region.

As the grandson of Mayangnokcha, a pioneer in Nagaland's education sector, Temjenmenba carries forward a legacy of service and commitment to the people. His vision encompasses unity, progress, and sustainable development for all communities in Nagaland.
